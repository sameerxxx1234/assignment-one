The Olympic Games, a global spectacle held every four years, unite athletes from across the globe in a celebration of sportsmanship and excellence. Originating in ancient Greece, the modern Olympics were revived in 1896 and have since become a symbol of international unity. The Games alternate between Summer and Winter editions, featuring a diverse array of sports and events. The iconic Olympic rings represent the five continents, and host cities are chosen through a competitive bidding process. The Opening and Closing Ceremonies, marked by the Parade of Nations and the lighting of the Olympic flame, highlight the spirit of camaraderie. Athletes strive for gold, silver, and bronze medals, embodying values such as friendship, respect, and determination. Beyond the Summer and Winter Games, the Paralympic Games showcase the remarkable abilities of athletes with disabilities, further emphasizing the inclusive and inspirational nature of the Olympic movement.

Iga Swiatek's Biography
Iga Swiatek

Iga Swiatek is a Polish professional tennis player. She has won one Grand Slam singles title at the 2020 French Open, becoming the first Polish player to win a Grand Slam singles title. Swiatek achieved a career-high singles ranking of world No. 1 on October 11, 2021.

Kaoru Mitoma's Biography
Kaoru Mitoma

Kaoru Mitoma is a Japanese professional footballer who plays as a midfielder. He represented Japan at the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo. Mitoma has been known for his agility, technical skills, and creativity on the field.

Gu Ailing Eileen's Biography
Gu Ailing Eileen

Gu Ailing Eileen is a Chinese-American freestyle skier. She competed for China at the 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing, winning gold medals in the women's halfpipe and slopestyle events. Gu's innovative style and technical prowess have made her a standout athlete in the world of freestyle skiing.

Lee Zii Jia's Biography
Lee Zii Jia

Lee Zii Jia is a Malaysian professional badminton player. He represented Malaysia at the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, where he reached the men's singles quarterfinals. Lee has won multiple titles on the international circuit and is known for his powerful smashes and agile footwork.
